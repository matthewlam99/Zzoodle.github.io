Spirulina Smoothies

The flavor of spirulina can be a little overpowering at first, so ease into it. The beautiful thing about smoothies is that you can mask it with strong, tasty flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Ripe Banana (Peeled)
  • 1 cup of Frozen Blueberries
  • 1 cup of Spinach
  • 1 cup of Kale
  • 3/4 cup of Water/ Almond Milk/ Coconut Water
  • 1 tbsp. of Chia Seeds
  • 1 tsp. Spirulina
  • 2 Medjool Pitted Dates (Optional)

Directions:

    1. Place all ingredients into a blender
    2. Blend until smooth
